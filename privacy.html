StudySnap — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

StudySnap is a Chrome extension that helps students answer study and quiz questions using AI. This policy explains exactly what data is accessed, how it is used, and what we never do.

What data we access

  • Screenshots: When you click "Capture Question", StudySnap captures a screenshot of the tab you are currently viewing. This image is used only to identify the question on screen and is sent directly to the AI provider you configured.
  • Your API key: You provide your own OpenAI or Anthropic API key. It is stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local and sent only to the AI provider you chose — never to any StudySnap server.
  • Answer history: A local log of your captured questions and answers is stored on your device only. It is never uploaded or shared.

How your data is used

  • Screenshots are sent directly from your browser to OpenAI or Anthropic using your own API key. StudySnap has no servers and never sees or stores your screenshots.
  • The AI provider processes your data under their own privacy policy: OpenAI Privacy Policy / Anthropic Privacy Policy.
  • FlowForge (the developer) collects no data whatsoever.

What we never do

  • We never collect, store, or transmit your screenshots to any server we control.
  • We never sell or share any data with third parties.
  • We never track your browsing history or activity.
  • We never access any tab other than the one you are actively viewing when you click Capture.

Local data storage

All data (API key, answer history, settings) is stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local. You can delete all stored data at any time from the History tab inside the extension, or by uninstalling StudySnap.

Permissions explained

  • activeTab: Captures a screenshot of the tab you are currently viewing — only when you click the extension button.
  • scripting: Injects the answer overlay onto the page so the answer appears directly on screen.
  • storage: Saves your API key, provider choice, and answer history locally on your device only.

Children's privacy

StudySnap does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone. All processing happens locally on your device or through your own AI provider account.
